MedicBot

About the project

MedicBot is a virtual assistant that using Artificial Intelligence is capable to guide doctors through a conversational experience to obtain all data necessary to generate the reports and recipes digitally using Anvil technologies, and send them to their emails.
Also, MedicBot is able to detect any moment if the person who is talking with is the doctor itself, through the bevahioral biometrics technologies of typingDNA. In case it detects something wrong, the doctor should enter its password or no report will be generated.
